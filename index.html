About Us

Magna Curia Chamber, Osun State University is a distinguished body within the College of Law, Ifetedo Campus. It is one of the four esteemed chambers in the Faculty of Law at UNIOSUN. This association proudly represents approximately one-quarter of the Faculty's student body, committed to the holistic development and welfare of its members. Our primary mission is to ensure the comprehensive well-being and empowerment of our members. We achieve this through the meticulous organization of a variety of legal, socio-cultural, and academic programs. These initiatives are specifically designed to equip our members with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the legal profession and to face future challenges with confidence. Magna Curia Chamber has consistently garnered accolades for its excellence and outstanding performance. Our association has been recognized at both state and national levels for its exceptional contributions and participation in various activities. These achievements underscore our commitment to excellence and our role as a beacon of success within the legal community.

Adetunji Michael Obadare

Lord Advocate

Michael Adetunji Obadare - Lord Advocate
×

Michael Adetunji Obadare (popularly known as MAO) is a penultimate law student of Faculty of Law, Osun State University. His interests span across areas such as Ip & Tech, Data privacy and protection, Cybersecurity and Responsible AI. Over the years, Michael has been able yo garner impressive out-of-classroom experience by completing internships with various firms including, AAA Chambers, Omaplex, JIIPCC etc . Furthermore he has held certain positions beyond the shore of his faculty which include but not limited to Director of programs, Simplex Law Academy Nigeria, Clerk, National Judicial Council, LAWSAN. Those who have worked with him have given positive reviews and recommendations about his drive for excellence and determination.
Having served as the Director of Litigation in the previous administration, Michael is currently serving as the Lord Advocate of Magna Curia Chamber, UNIOSUN.
Under Michael's visionary guidance at Magna Curia Chamber, Osun State University, his exceptional leadership has created a thriving environment where team members can flourish. With a deep understanding of the impact leadership can have, he cultivates a culture of inclusivity, actively listening to diverse perspectives, embracing innovative ideas, and nurturing individual growth, allowing each member to reach new heights.

Ogunkoya Francis Ifeoluwa

Deputy Lord Advocate

Francis Ifeoluwa Ogunkoya - Deputy Lord advocate
×

Francis Ifeoluwa Ogunkoya is a law undergraduate from Osun State University, Nigeria. He is passionate about Media/Entertainment Law, Corporate and Commercial Law Practice, and Intellectual Property Law, amongst others. His enthusiasm for learning and ambition have led him to explore the tech world from a young age. He is a graphics designer, a programmer, a software developer, and a certified Microsoft professional. He served as the President of the Press Crew Organization and was the Director of Media and Publicity of the Legal Torch Initiative (LTI), one of the leading communities for Nigerian law students. He has held various leadership positions, demonstrating his capabilities as a leader. He is resilient and dedicated to carrying out his duties with excellence.
Francis has developed strong advocacy and public speaking skills. He is an active member of the moot and mock team, participating in various competitions. His passion for advocacy led to his appointment as the Court Bailiff of the Judicial Council of Law Students Society, UNIOSUN. He has experience in brief writing, memorial drafting, and client counseling, demonstrating his strong interest in litigation. He is a proud human rights activist and a catalyst for positive change, always willing to take bold steps to improve a situation.
In his leisure time, he enjoys surfing the internet, reading, debating, teaching, and learning. Francis has a positive team spirit and possesses outstanding stamina to fit into teamwork effectively. He is a motivational speaker and a moderator. He is an avid reader, researcher, and tech enthusiast. He is open to internships, collaborations, mentorship, and development.
